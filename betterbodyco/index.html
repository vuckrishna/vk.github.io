What is Menopause?

Women have always been known to have great intuition naturally when it comes to noticing changes in the body.

These changes include Emotional, Physical, Mental.

Such as Drastic Mood Swings, Frequent Outbursts.

These complicated changes can indicate the start of Menopause.

Menopause is a transition period in which the body winds down our reproductive abilities and is not to be taken lightly.

3 Stages of Menopause

Stage 1

Perimenopause

- Usually start at age of 40.
- But can also start as early as 30.
- this reduces our fertility rate.
- Women planning to start a family should hurry.

- Change in duration of mentrual cycle.
- Often having long gaps b/w periods or spotting b/w 2 cycles.
- this leads to frustration for lack of info for your next date & how heavy the flow will be.

- Includes drastic & frequent mood swings
- Along with Insomnia or Hot flashes.
- this causes irritation & losing energy.
- Feels tried even after simple taks & naps.

Stage 2

Menopause

- Minimal production od estrogen.
- Getting pregnent in significantly hardly in this stage.
- Use of hormonal therapy or IVF required for childbirth.
- Women start feeling low & not to get out of bed.

- Body starts to feel warm suddenly, with flashed red skin & increased heart rate.
- Followed by suddenly feeling cold.
- this include waling up in middle of night.
- turning the fan on max even the aircon is on full blast.

- Eating one slice of cake could increase weight.
- Due to reduction in metabolic rate.
- notice hair thinning and dry skin no matter the amount of conditioner or lotion you put on.
- Feels tried even after simple taks & naps.

Stage 3

Postmenopause

- Increases drastically as ovaries shut down.
- Lower estrogen level in the body.
- Start noticing lower energy level.
- Wanting to sleep in by 7/8pm.

- Includes diabeties, joint pains, esteroporosis, cholestrol & heart disease.
- Visit to doctor is like drawing a lottery ticket.
- As it is nessesary to manitain a healthy lifestyle..

- Forgetting car keys or deadlines seem to increase over time.
- However these are temporary symptoms.
- reduced in brain derived neurotrophic factors that cause depression & anxiety.
