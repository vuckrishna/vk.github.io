About the Summit

The Leadership Summit 2018 brings together community leaders, organisers and volunteers who are interested in growing and empowering a strong community.

The event is done in a conference-style where everyone who attends is welcome to lead and contribute sessions on any topic that is relevant. These discussion sessions allows participants to interact directly and share their thoughts and experiences. They will be able to share their ideas and questions. This event provides unlimited networking opportunities.
